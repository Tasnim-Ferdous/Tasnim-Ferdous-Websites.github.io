Cheese and Mushroom Toast

  • Time: 1-2 min
Ingredients Quantity
White bread (Sandwich loaf) 3 slices
Mushrooms in oil, finely chopped 100 g
Cheese (Grated) 20 g

Step 1: After cutting the crusts off the bread, spread the finely chopped mushrooms over each slice.
Step 2: Sprinkle with grated cheese and cut each slice into four.
Step 3: Lay the pieces in a microwaveable dish and cook at 750 W for 1-2 minutes.

Asparagus and Cheese Soup

  • Time: 28 -35 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Fresh asparagus 750 g
Onions, thinly sliced 2
Stock 1 litre
Olive oil 3 tablespoons
Cream cheese 125 g
Salt As needed
Pepper As needed

Step 1: Place the oil and onion in a microwaveable dish.
Step 2: Cook uncovered for 3-4 minutes at 750 W.
Step 3: Cut off the asparagus tips and set aside.
Step 4: Cut the asparagus spears into 5 cm slices, making sure you discard the woody end, and combine with the onion. Cook uncovered for 5 minutes at 750 W.
Step 5: Add the stock, cover, and cook for a further 15-20 minutes at 500 W, stirring from time to time.
Step 6: Stir in the cream cheese and blend until you are left with a smooth creamy mixture.
Step 7: Add the asparagus tips. Heat at 750 W for 5-6 minutes. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
An extra special touch: If you like the flavour, when you add the asparagus tips you may also sprinkle in two tablespoons of dill.

Tomato Soup

  • Time: 26 -33 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Ripe tomatoes 750 g
Olive oil 2 tablespoons
Onion, thinly sliced 1 litre
Clove garlic, crushed 1
Tomato sauce 1 tablespoon
Carrot, finely chopped 1
Vegetable or chicken stock 500 ml
Sugar 1 teaspoon

Step 1: Make a slit in the tomatoes and microwave them at 750 W for 6-8 minutes, until they are easy to peel.
Step 2: Peel and dice the tomatoes.
Step 3: Place the oil, onion and garlic in a shallow microwaveable tray.
Step 4: Cook uncovered for 3-4 minutes at 750 W.
Step 5: Add the tomato sauce, the diced tomato and the chopped carrot, cover and cook for a further 4-5 minutes at 750 W.
Step 6: Add the sugar while stirring. Cover and cook for 8-10 minutes at 750 W. Stir until you are left with a smooth, creamy mixture.
Step 7: Pour over the vegetable or chicken stock and cook, uncovered, for 5-6 minutes at 750 W.
An extra special touch: Add fresh basil leaves and freshly ground pepper, or scramble some eggs and add them to the soup before serving.
